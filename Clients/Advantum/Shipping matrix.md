# Shipping Matrix – Advantum

This document provides a structured overview of shipping regions, delivery times, costs, and operational shipping rules. It serves as a reference for consistent and accurate customer support responses.

---

## 1. Shipping Regions

Advantum currently ships to the following regions:

- Netherlands
- Belgium

Shipping to other countries or regions is not available.

---

## 2. Delivery Times Per Region

Estimated delivery times are as follows:

- Netherlands: 1–3 business days
- Belgium: 1–3 business days

Delivery times are estimates and may be affected by external factors.

---

## 3. Shipping Costs

Shipping costs are charged as a flat rate per order:

- Netherlands: €5.95
- Belgium: €8.95

Advantum does not offer free shipping unless explicitly stated otherwise.

---

## 4. Shipping Carriers

Orders are shipped using the following carriers:

- PostNL
- MyParcelNL

Carrier selection is handled internally and may vary per order.

---

## 5. Tracking Information

Tracking information is provided automatically:

- A tracking link is sent via email once the order is fulfilled
- Tracking updates are managed by the carrier

If tracking information is not received, customers should contact support.

---

## 6. Handling Time

- Orders placed on business days are **shipped on the same day**, provided payment is successfully completed
- Orders placed on weekends or public holidays are shipped on the next business day

---

## 7. Delays & Exceptions

Delivery delays may occur due to:

- Public holidays
- High-volume periods
- Carrier-related issues
- Weather conditions

These delays are outside the direct control of Advantum.

---

## 8. Non-Delivery Rules

### Undeliverable Shipments
If a shipment cannot be delivered and is returned to the sender:
- The customer should contact customer support
- Further steps will be handled according to company policy

### Lost Shipments
If a shipment appears to be lost:
- Advantum will initiate an investigation with the carrier
- Resolution depends on the outcome of the carrier investigation

### Stolen Packages
Advantum is not responsible for packages marked as delivered by the carrier. Customers should contact support to review the situation.

---

## 9. Additional Notes

- All shipping rules apply equally to all customers
- Shipping information must always be aligned with official policies
- The assistant must never promise delivery guarantees

---

End of shipping matrix.
