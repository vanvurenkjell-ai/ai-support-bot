# Advantum – Policies

This document outlines the official policies of Advantum. These policies apply to all orders placed through the Advantum webshop unless stated otherwise.

---

## 1. Shipping / Fulfillment Policy

### Shipping Regions
Advantum ships to:
- The Netherlands
- Belgium

Shipping to other regions is not supported unless explicitly agreed upon in writing.

### Order Processing & Dispatch
All orders placed on business days are **dispatched on the same day**, provided payment has been successfully completed.

Orders placed on weekends or public holidays are dispatched on the next business day.

### Delivery Time
- Estimated delivery time: **1–3 business days**

Delivery times are estimates and not guarantees. External factors such as carrier delays or peak periods may affect delivery.

### Shipping Costs
- Netherlands: €5.95 per order  
- Belgium: €8.95 per order  

Advantum **does not offer free shipping**, regardless of order value or promotions, unless explicitly stated otherwise.

### Shipping Method
Orders are shipped via professional parcel carriers. Tracking information is provided where available.

---

## 2. Return & Refund Policy

### Right of Withdrawal (EU Consumer Law)
Customers have the right to withdraw from the purchase agreement within **14 days** of receiving the product, without stating a reason.

The withdrawal period starts on the day after the customer receives the final product of the order.

### Return Conditions
- Return shipping costs are borne by the customer.
- Original shipping costs are **not refunded**.
- Products may only be used to the extent necessary for inspection, comparable to a physical retail store.
- If the product is used beyond inspection, Advantum may deduct depreciation from the refund.

### How to Start a Return
The customer must notify Advantum of the withdrawal via an unambiguous written statement (e.g. email).

After notification, the product must be returned within **14 days**.

### Refunds
Refunds are processed within **14 days** after withdrawal notification, provided that:
- The product has been received, or
- Proof of return shipment has been submitted

Refunds are issued using the original payment method unless otherwise agreed.

### Withdrawal Exceptions
Certain products may be excluded from the right of withdrawal. This will always be clearly stated on the relevant product page.

---

## 3. Cancellation Policy

Orders can only be cancelled **before they are shipped**.

Once an order has been dispatched, cancellation is no longer possible and the return procedure applies.

Customers must contact customer support as soon as possible to request cancellation.

---

## 4. Warranty / Guarantee Policy

Advantum complies with all applicable Dutch and EU consumer warranty laws.

### Legal Conformity
Customers are entitled to products that conform to the agreement, meaning the product:
- Matches the description
- Is suitable for normal use
- Meets reasonable expectations of quality

### Warranty Claims
If a product is defective or non-conforming upon delivery, the customer must contact Advantum within a reasonable timeframe.

Depending on the situation, Advantum may offer:
- Repair
- Replacement
- Partial or full refund

Damage caused by misuse, improper handling, or normal wear and tear is excluded.

## 7. Privacy & Data Policy (Summary)

### General
Advantum values customer privacy and processes personal data in accordance with the **General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR)**.

We:
- Clearly define purposes before processing personal data
- Collect only data necessary for those purposes
- Request explicit consent where required
- Apply appropriate security measures
- Respect all data subject rights

### Order Processing
To process orders, Advantum uses:
- Contact details (name, address, email, phone number)
- Order and payment details
- Account information (if applicable)
- Customer support communications

Order-related data is stored for up to **two years** after order completion, unless longer retention is required by tax law.

### Third Parties
Personal data may be shared with essential service providers, including:
- Payment providers: Visa, Mastercard, iDEAL, PayPal, Bancontact
- Shipping providers: MyParcelNL, PostNL
- Shopify (website and infrastructure)

Some providers are located outside the EU (e.g. Shopify, Visa, Mastercard). Appropriate safeguards are applied.

### Newsletter & Marketing
Customers are added to the newsletter **only with explicit consent**.

Used data:
- Name
- Email address
- Phone number (if provided)

The newsletter has no fixed frequency and is sent only when relevant. Each email includes an unsubscribe option.

### Advertising
Advertising communications are sent exclusively via the newsletter. Customers can opt out at any time.

### Cookies & Analytics
Advantum uses cookies to support and improve website functionality and user experience. Cookie usage related to Shopify can be reviewed via Shopify’s cookie policy.

Cookies can be disabled in browser settings, though this may impact site functionality.

### Security
Advantum applies appropriate technical and organizational security measures. No system is fully secure, and absolute security cannot be guaranteed.
