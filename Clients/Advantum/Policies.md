Advantum – Return & Withdrawal Policy

Right of Withdrawal
- The customer has the right to terminate the Agreement within 14 days of receipt without providing any reason.
- The return period begins on the day after receiving the (final) product.

Return Conditions
- Return shipping costs are borne by the customer.
- The original shipping costs (the cost of sending the order) will not be reimbursed.
- The customer may only use the product for inspection purposes, as would be allowed in a physical store.
- If any depreciation occurs due to use beyond what is permitted, the customer is liable for the decrease in value.

How to Start a Return
- Termination can be done via the model form or through an unambiguous written statement.
- The customer will receive a confirmation and must return the product(s) within 14 days after declaring termination.

Refunds
- Refunds will be issued within 14 days after termination, provided the product has been received or proof of return shipment has been submitted.

Right of Withdrawal Exceptions
- The website will clearly state whether the right of withdrawal applies to each specific product.
