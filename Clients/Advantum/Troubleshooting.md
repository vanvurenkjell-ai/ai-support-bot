This document defines how the assistant should handle common issues related to products, orders, shipping, payments, and technical problems. If an issue cannot be resolved with the steps below, the assistant must always redirect the customer to human support.

---

## 1. Product Issues

### Omnirope

**Issue:** The Omnirope feels uncomfortable during use  
**Fix:**  
Make sure the Omnirope is not being tightly gripped. The arms should be placed into the loops so the wrists can rest on the material. This allows the intended biomechanics of the Omnirope to function correctly.

If discomfort persists, advise the customer to review the product usage information on the website.

---

### Glute Strap

**Issue:** The Glute Strap does not fit around the ankle  
**Fix:**  
Refer the customer to the instruction video available on the product page of the Glute Strap on the website.

**Issue:** The Glute Strap slides during the exercise or feels unstable  
**Fix:**  
Ensure that the heel strap is properly secured. If the customer is unsure whether the setup is correct, refer them to the instruction video on the product page.

---

### Swipe Grip

**Issue:** A lifting strap does not fit around the Swipe Grip  
**Fix:**  
Ensure the rope of the Swipe Grip is positioned in the side notch of the grip. This creates an opening where the lifting strap can be placed.  
If this does not resolve the issue, refer the customer to the instruction video on the website.

---

## 2. Order Issues

For all order-related issues, the assistant must redirect the customer to human support.

**Issues include:**
- Tracking link not working  
- Wrong product received  
- Incorrect address entered by the customer  
- Order number unknown  
- Any other order-related issue the assistant cannot resolve  

**Fix:**  
Instruct the customer to contact customer support at:  
**info@advantum.nl**

---

## 3. Shipping Issues

Shipping-related issues require direct human review.

**Issues include:**
- Delays during transit  
- No tracking updates  
- Package marked as delivered but not received  

**Fix:**  
Redirect the customer to customer support at:  
**info@advantum.nl**

---

## 4. Payment & Checkout Issues

### Card Declined

**Fix:**  
First, confirm that the selected payment method is accepted by Advantum.  
If it is accepted, advise the customer to contact their bank or payment provider.  
If the issue persists, redirect the customer to:  
**info@advantum.nl**

---

### Payment Pending

**Fix:**  
This situation is not applicable. Advantum does not offer post-payment or pay-later options. Orders are only processed after successful payment.

---

### Discount Code Not Applying

**Fix:**  
Check whether the discount code is active according to the `promotions_and_discounts.md` file.  
If the code is active, ask the customer to verify that the code is entered correctly.  
If the issue remains unresolved, redirect the customer to:  
**info@advantum.nl**

---

## 5. Account Issues

Advantum does **not** use customer accounts.

This section is not applicable.

---

## 6. Technical Issues

Technical issues must always be escalated.

**Issues include:**
- Website not loading  
- Product page not working  
- Checkout errors  

**Fix:**  
Redirect the customer to customer support at:  
**info@advantum.nl**

---

## 7. When to Escalate

The assistant must escalate to human support in **all** of the following situations:

- The issue cannot be resolved with the steps above  
- Multiple fixes have failed  
- Product defects are suspected  
- Wrong items or shipping problems occur  
- Refunds or disputes are mentioned  
- Legal or policy-related concerns arise  
- The customer shows frustration, discomfort, or anger  

**Escalation method:**  
Always redirect the customer to:  
**info@advantum.nl**
