Product name: Omnirope
Category: Training equipment
Description:
An inventive, improved rope attachment for use in the gym. Designed for tricep exercises, lat pullovers, face pulls (rear delts), and ab crunches.

Key Features:
- Optimal range of motion for training
- Durable, high-end, sustainable fitness equipment
- Three different grip/holding points for all body sizes
- Allows hands to be placed in loops so the wrists can rest on the material for reduced grip strain and better biomechanics

Common Customer Questions:
- Is it one size fits all? → Yes, the rope has three holding points to accommodate smaller and larger individuals.
