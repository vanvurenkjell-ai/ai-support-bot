# Company Overview – Advantum

This document provides a clear and accurate overview of Advantum. It is used to ensure the assistant represents the company consistently, correctly, and without assumptions.

---

## 1. Company Name

**Advantum VOF**

---

## 2. What the Company Does

Advantum sells **physical fitness and training products**.

### Short Company Description
Advantum is a fitness accessories brand focused on making high-end training equipment accessible to everyone. The company designs and sells physical products that aim to improve training quality, comfort, and execution.

---

## 3. Mission & Purpose

Advantum exists to give everyone the opportunity to improve their training experience. Not everyone has access to high-end gyms with premium equipment, but that should not mean settling for minimal or suboptimal tools.

By offering high-end fitness equipment in an accessible and affordable way, Advantum aims to positively influence training quality and help individuals move closer to their physical goals through better equipment and better execution.

---

## 4. Target Audience

Advantum serves a broad fitness-oriented audience, including:

- Recreational gym users  
- Serious strength trainers and bodybuilders  
- Beginners and experienced athletes  
- Health-conscious individuals  
- Students, professionals, and general gym members  

The products are designed to be usable and beneficial across different experience levels.

---

## 5. Unique Selling Points (USPs)

Advantum differentiates itself through:

- High-end fitness equipment that is accessible to everyone  
- Products designed **for athletes and by athletes**  
- Strong focus on optimizing biomechanics to improve muscle activation and execution  
- Use of premium, durable materials designed for long-term use  

---

## 6. Brand Values

Core values of Advantum:

- **Performance** – products are designed to improve training execution  
- **Discipline** – consistency and structure over shortcuts  
- **Quality** – premium materials and thoughtful design  
- **Education** – empowering users through understanding and proper usage  

---

## 7. Company Background

Advantum was founded in **2023** by two friends, **Jort and Kjell**, who shared a strong passion for fitness and training. After years of training and experimenting with a wide range of gym equipment, they identified recurring limitations in standard fitness accessories.

This led to the creation of Advantum: a brand focused on improving overall gym equipment quality for every individual, regardless of gym or experience level.

By **2025**, Advantum remains driven by the same passion for training and performance. Kjell has taken this commitment further as an active bodybuilder, preparing for his first stage appearance in April 2025. Advantum is supported by an athlete team consisting of multiple bodybuilders, reinforcing the principle of *for athletes, by athletes*.

---

## 8. Industry Information

Advantum operates as a:

- Fitness & training accessories brand  
- Performance- and biomechanics-focused brand  

The company emphasizes proper movement, control, and long-term training quality rather than trends or hype.

---

## 9. Additional Information

- Advantum does not make medical, health, or performance guarantees  
- The company avoids hype-based marketing and exaggerated claims  
- All communication prioritizes clarity, honesty, and realistic expectations
